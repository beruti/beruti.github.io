Work Completed at AKQA

AKQA is an International Advertising Agency specialising in digital product and service design. Future Academy was a client-winning initiative to showcase innovation within AKQA. I produced 4 projects whilst there: for Jamie Oliver, Tag Heuer, Google and AKQA itself.

Sherpa

People aren’t following recipes. Whether its because its too much effort, the experience isn’t engaging enough or the list of ingredients required is too long or extravagant, they aren’t doing it. It effects our health and stunts our knowledge and appreciation of food and its provenance. As Sherpa, we aim to revolutionise the world of food by encouraging more people to make healthy, delicious and nutritious recipes a natural part of their lifestyle. We want to define what it means to be the world’s best recipe service, one meal at a time.

Google Move

Google challenged us to create an original concept that could show the power of their machine learning platform TensorFlow. Given such an open brief the team wanted to concentrate on a huge problem. We were shocked by the insight that, after the death of a loved one and divorce, moving house is the third most stressful experience of modern life. We set out to simplify and personalise this process. We believe that, with TensorFlow, such an arcane process can be rendered transparent and its most painful points automated using current open data standards and the libraries of the machine learning ecosystem.

Next Steps

At first glance it seems fantastic that 47% of school leavers, more than ever before, now go on to higher education in the UK but when we look closer we find that, despite their mountains of debt, 59% of graduates go into jobs they don’t need a degree for. Next Steps is an intelligent assistant, designed to help graduates find and enter meaningful jobs in the age of the portfolio career.
